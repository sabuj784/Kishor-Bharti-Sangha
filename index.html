About Us

Kishor Bharti Sangha has been a beacon of culture and community spirit since its inception 75 years ago. Founded by a group of dedicated individuals, our club has grown into a pillar of our community, known for our vibrant Durga Puja celebrations and various social initiatives.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of unity and cultural heritage among our members and the broader community. We aim to celebrate and preserve our rich traditions while promoting inclusivity and community service.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Kishor Bharti Sangha continues to thrive as a cultural hub, inspiring the next generation to embrace and uphold our traditions. We strive to expand our reach and impact, contributing positively to society through various cultural, educational, and social initiatives.
